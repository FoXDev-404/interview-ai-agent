\section{API Endpoints}
\label{app:api}
% ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Table~\ref{tab:api_endpoints} lists every REST route exposed by the system.
All endpoints reside under the Next.js \texttt{app/api/} directory and are
protected by the session-cookie middleware unless marked \emph{Public}.

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\caption{System API Endpoints}
\label{tab:api_endpoints}
\begin{tabular}{|p{4.2cm}|p{1.6cm}|p{1.5cm}|p{6cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Endpoint} & \textbf{Method} & \textbf{Auth} & \textbf{Description} \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/auth/session}       & POST & Public  & Exchange Firebase ID token for a session cookie \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/auth/logout}        & POST & Public  & Clear the session cookie \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/auth/check}         & GET  & Cookie  & Validate session and return user identity \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/resume/analyze}     & POST & Cookie  & ATS analysis -- match resume against job description \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/speech/analyze}     & POST & Cookie  & Transcription + speech-coach scoring \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/speech/realtime-token} & GET & Cookie & Generate AssemblyAI real-time token \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/coding/execute}     & POST & Cookie  & Execute candidate code in a sandboxed runtime \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/coding/validate}    & POST & Cookie  & Run test-case suite against submitted code \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/contact}            & POST & Public  & Submit contact form (dispatched via Nodemailer) \\
\hline
\texttt{/api/support}            & POST & Public  & Create a support ticket \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{table}
